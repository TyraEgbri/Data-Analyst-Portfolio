## Project Overview
This mini project presents an interactive HR dashboard analysing employee demographics.

## Key Features
- Bar chart showing employee count by gender
- Pie chart displaying age distribution
- KPI card showing total employees
- Slicer to filter results by gender

## Insights
1. Male employees form the majority of the workforce (7 out of 11).
2. The average employee age is approximately 40 years.
3. A significant proportion of employees are aged 40+, indicating the need for succession and workforce planning.
