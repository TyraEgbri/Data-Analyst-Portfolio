# Shopping Behaviour Sales Dashboard 

## Sales by Product Category
- Clothing had the highest purchase amount compared to accessories, footwear, and outwear. This suggests that clothing is the most popular among customers.

## Age Group Purchases
- The 50+ age group contributed the largest share of purchases, while the 18-25 age group had the smallest share. This indicates that older customers are driving more sales.

## Sales by Season
- Fall was the top season for purchases, showing a peak in revenue during that time. This suggests seasonal trends that might influence marketing strategies.
