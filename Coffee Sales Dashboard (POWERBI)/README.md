# Coffee Sales Power BI Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard visualises coffee sales data for a café, including customer transactions, coffee types, and sales trends. The goal is to provide a clear, interactive overview for management to make informed business decisions.

**Dataset Summary:**
- Features include:
  - Hour of the day
  - Payment method
  - Money spent
  - Coffee type
  - Time of day (morning, afternoon, evening)
  - Weekday
  - Month
  - Exact date and time of purchase

---

## Dashboard Features
1. **KPIs / Summary Cards**
   - Average spend per transaction: **£31.90**
   - Total sales, total transactions, and other key metrics

2. **Charts**
   - **Top-selling drinks**: Shows the most popular coffee
   - **Sales by hour**: Identifies peak sales times
   - **Sales by weekday**: Shows busiest and quietest days
   - **Sales by month**: Displays monthly trends

3. **Slicers**
   - Interactive filters for month, weekday and coffee type
   - Single-select for simplicity and user-friendliness

---

## Insights

1. **Top-selling drinks**
   - **Latte, Americano with milk, Cappuccino**
   - *Why it matters:* Shows customer favorites
   - *Recommendation:* Ensure stock and consider promotions

2. **Lowest-selling drinks**
   - **Espresso, Cortado, Cocoa**
   - *Why it matters:* These drinks may tie up resources
   - *Recommendation:* Consider promotion strategies or menu adjustment

3. **Sales by Hour**
   - Peak sales occur at **10 AM in the morning**
   - *Why it matters:* Helps plan staffing and preparation during the busiest time
   - *Recommendation:* Ensure sufficient staff and inventory ready for morning peak

4. **Sales by Weekday**
   - **Tuesday** is busiest, **Sunday** is quietest
   - *Why it matters:* Shows customer patterns
   - *Recommendation:* Adjust staffing and promotions accordingly

5. **Sales by Month**
   - **March** has the highest sales
   - *Why it matters:* Highlights seasonal trends
   - *Recommendation:* Prepare for peak months with inventory and marketing campaigns

---

## Notes
- Average spend is formatted as **£31.90** for clarity.
- Null values in the "Hour of Day" column were removed for accurate peak hour analysis.
- Dashboard designed for **non-technical users**, with clear visualizations and intuitive filters.
